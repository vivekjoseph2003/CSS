ABOUT WATCHES

A watch is a portable timepiece, worn on the wrist as a wristwatch or carried in a pocket as a pocket watch. Modern watches can be mechanical, powered by a mainspring, or electronic, often using a quartz crystal for accuracy. Beyond telling time, watches are also a significant fashion accessory, with modern smartwatches offering additional digital features like fitness tracking and smartphone integration.
