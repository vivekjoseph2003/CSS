NOTICE BOX

The Annual Day celebration is one of the most awaited events of the year, as it gives our students a wonderful platform to showcase their talents, creativity, and hard work. This year’s celebration promises to be an unforgettable experience filled with music, dance, drama, and inspiring performances by our students from all grades. The theme of the program reflects our school’s commitment to excellence, teamwork, and holistic development.The event will begin with a warm welcome address, followed by the lighting of the ceremonial lamp by our honorable chief guest, Mr. Harry Maguire, and other dignitaries. After the inaugural ceremony, the cultural performances will commence, featuring a wide range of activities including classical and contemporary dances, musical renditions, skits, and a special performance dedicated to our school’s achievements throughout the year. Each performance has been thoughtfully prepared by our talented students under the guidance of our dedicated teachers.In addition to the cultural presentations, the Annual Report will be presented by our Principal, highlighting the academic and extracurricular accomplishments of our students and staff. This will be followed by the prize distribution ceremony, where meritorious students will be recognized for their excellence in academics, sports, and various co-curricular activities. Their dedication and hard work truly reflect the spirit and values of our institution.We encourage all parents and family members to join us in celebrating the growth and success of our students. Your presence and encouragement mean a lot to them and will make this occasion even more memorable. Refreshments will be served after the program, and we look forward to sharing this joyous day with our entire school community.Let us come together on 31st December 2025, at 9:00 a.m., to celebrate our students’ achievements and welcome the new year with enthusiasm, joy, and pride. Together, let’s make this Annual Day a grand success and a day to remember for everyone!
